methodology for calculating and analyzing our raw data


  • only restaurants with scores from at least two raters receive an aggregate score
  • scores are not limited to comparison to only DC restaurants
  • scores are not limited to comparison to only restaurants within the same type
    • i.e., by nature, it is virturally imposisble for a dive bar to receive a 5
  • scores may be based on outdated information (prior chefs, suppliers, etc.)
    • noted in comments where possible
